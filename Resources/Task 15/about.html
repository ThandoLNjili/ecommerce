Who are we and what do we stand for?

Can's Addict was founded in 2004 by Joe Acres from his home in Sandton, and it has grown from strength to strength and established itself as more than just a hit start-up.
Can's Addict focuses on some key aspects about it's products. Read about it here in our mission and visions statement:

Mission and Vision

  • To ensure that customers only receieve the best quality in studio headphones, delivered to their doorstep.
  • To validate the products from our suppliers before shipment.
  • To ensure that shipment is carried out in a fashion that is timely and safely to preserve the product at all costs.
  • To cater for and exceed the needs and budgets of our customers
  • To offer repairs and replacements of parts up to standards, should they be within their guarantee period.
